Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to help you decide quickly and secure your audit with confidence.

Is this suitable for small landlords with just one or two properties?

Yes. Small landlords often recover the strongest percentage gains because they have never had this level of specialist review. Even one property can carry significant undocumented improvements, and the audit often repays its cost several times over.

What if I already have an accountant?

Your existing accountant handles annual compliance. We add specialist field audit work that most firms do not provide, including on-site inspection, CGT documentation, and risk-led tax strategy. Many accountants refer landlords to us for this exact reason.

What if I own a larger portfolio across multiple properties?

We structure portfolio audits to prioritise highest-value properties first, standardise evidence across units, and map actions by impact. You get a practical sequence that improves tax efficiency and compliance at scale.

Does this replace my annual accounts?

No. The Field Audit supports your annual accounts by addressing property tax strategy, CGT evidence, and compliance risk. You continue normal year-end filings while we strengthen the areas that most often create expensive surprises.

How exactly does this protect my CGT position?

When you sell, HMRC allows qualifying improvement costs only when you prove them. We document each eligible improvement to HMRC standards, so you reduce taxable gain with evidence instead of estimates.

Is this only for limited company landlords?

No. We work with individual landlords, partnerships, and limited-company structures. We adapt the audit to your setup and identify the planning actions that best reduce tax and risk in your circumstances.
